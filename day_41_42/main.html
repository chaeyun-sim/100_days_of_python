Chaeyun Sim

electroacoustic composer

Chae yun Sim is a composer and a sound designer. Her compositional interest includes the combination of electronic and experimental acoustic sounds, including the materialization of noise structure on conceptual musical spaces. Her works have been performed in many festivals such as SICMF, DICMF, FEST-M, etc. She majored music composition at EWHA Womans University, and now currently studying electro-acoustic music composition at Korea National University of Arts.
